The Heritage

Chef Li's Legacy

Precision is not just a technique; it is a form of respect. For over forty years, Chef Li has dedicated his life to the mastery of dim sum. Beginning his journey in the bustling teahouses of Guangzhou, he learned that the soul of a dumpling lies in the warmth of the hands that shape it.

"We do not use machines," Chef Li insists. "A machine has no memory, no feeling. The dough must be felt, the filling must be balanced by instinct." It is this unwavering commitment to tradition that earned Dragon Dumpling its Michelin recognition in 2019.

Every morning at 4 AM, the kitchen comes alive. Steam rises, knives chop in rhythm, and the day begins anew—honoring the past with every basket served.
