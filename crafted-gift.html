Our Story

Where Tradition
Meets Intention

Crafted Gift began in a small workshop with a simple belief: that the act of giving deserves the same care as the gift itself. Each piece in our collection is made by hand, using time-honoured techniques passed down through generations of artisans.

We partner with independent craftspeople from around the world — ceramicists in the English countryside, leather workers in Tuscany, weavers in Oaxaca. Every collaboration is built on mutual respect and a shared commitment to quality over quantity.

No mass production. No shortcuts. Just honest, beautiful objects made to be given and kept.

Our Process

From Workshop to Doorstep

01
Step One

Source

We seek out the finest natural materials — raw cotton, unfinished leather, mineral-rich clays — ethically sourced from trusted suppliers across three continents.

02
Step Two

Craft

Each piece is shaped, fired, stitched, or woven by hand in our artisans' workshops. No machines, no moulds — just skilled hands and generations of knowledge.

03
Step Three

Inspect

Every item is carefully examined for quality before it earns our maker's mark. We accept nothing less than exceptional craftsmanship.

04
Step Four

Wrap

Presented in sustainable, beautifully designed packaging — because the experience of unwrapping should be just as memorable as the gift inside.
