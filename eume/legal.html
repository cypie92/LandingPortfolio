Booking & Cancellations

To secure your date, a non-refundable retainer of 50% of the total estimated cost is required upon signing the contract. The remaining balance is due 30 days prior to the event date.

6+ Months Out

Full refund of any payments made excluding the non-refundable retainer fee.

3-6 Months Out

50% refund of payments made excluding the retainer. 50% of remaining balance may be due.

30 Days Out

No refunds. Full payment is required as materials have been sourced.

Force Majeure

In cases of Acts of God, we offer rescheduling options within 12 months, subject to availability.
